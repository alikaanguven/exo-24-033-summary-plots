*Stop-antistop (sbottom-antisbottom) production cross sections* in proton-proton collisions, <latex> pp \to \tilde t \tilde t^*/\tilde b \tilde b^* </latex>, at 13 !TeV, computed at NNLO<sub>approx</sub>+NNLL in the limit of large squark (other than the stop/sbottom) and gluino masses (decoupled regime). No top quarks are considered in the initial state. The PDF set is !PDF4LHC21_40_pdfas (LHAPDF ID 93300).

Uncertainties (corresponding to PDF+<latex>\alpha_s</latex> and scale uncertainties as well as the uncertainty related to the choice of the stop/sbottom mixing angle added in quadrature) on theory predictions are symmetrized.
